    Activity 2.1. Recreating Adaptive Traits in Exotic Germplasm to Support Pre-breeding
    • Utilize CRISPR-Cas9 genome-editing technologies to domesticate exotic genetic resources prior to hybrid development. We propose to design and evaluate methods to identify genetic resources with high general combining ability (GCA) by combining the methods outlined above: gene editing and hybrid wheat.
